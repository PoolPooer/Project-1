Waterford Based Alternative Indie Rock

Formed in Waterford in 2020, Deaf Monkey play a brand of high energy punchy guitar driven indie rock/pop.

Deaf Monkey are Dermot Quinn, ( Vocals / Guitar) Andrew "Chewie" Cusack, (Bass / Vocals) Kieran O'Halloran, (Drums)
Sean O'Sullivan, ( Guitar / Keys / Madness) and Paul Power (Guitar).
